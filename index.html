Oclock
A Clean Pomodoro Timer

A Pomodoro focus & productivity timer to help you accomplish your goals. Oclock uses a dark and distraction-free UI So you can comfortably put your phone on your desk while working.

Explore Now

Features

The main features of the app

Pausable timer

You can pause the timer if anything unexpected happens so it wont screw up your working rhythm.



Dark & Clean Interface

We designed the app to be comfortable to look at and work as a 'desk companion'.



Customizable Work & Break Time

The standard 25,5 & 15 minute sessions are not perfect for everyone. That's why we made it possible to customize these intervals to fit your working style.

Ad-free

The app is completely ad-free & also free to download




The Pomodoro Technique

Oclock uses the standard Pomodoro technique, Work-Break 4x and then a longer break. The app switches automatically between the work timer and break timer and can send you notifications so you don't even have to look at your phone in between.
